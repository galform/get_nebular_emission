# UNITS:

[Z###] = dimensionless; Z017.txt corresponds to Zism=M(Z)/M(tot)=0.017
[log(Us)] = dimensionless
[xid] = dimensionless; dust-to-metal mass ratio
[nh] = [cm**-3] ; hydrogen density
[(C/O)/(C/O)sol]= dimensionless where (C/O)sol=0.44
[mup] = [M_sun]; upper mass cutoff of the IMF
[intensities of emission lines] = [Lbol_sun per unit SFR in 10^8yr]; calculated assuming constant SFR during 10^8yr and a Chabrier IMF. Lbol_sun = 3.826E+33egr s^-1
